We are actively looking for volunteers to help build and sustain Nova VNDB. All positions are remote and voluntary.

## How to Apply

Please reach out via the founder's email: [amaki@plr.moe](mailto:amaki@plr.moe).

Discord or telegram will be available soon.

## Available Positions

### Web Developer

We are looking for backend developers and frontend developers to build and maintain the Nova VNDB website.

For backend developers, we are looking for someone who is familiar with the following technologies:

- Rust
- axum
- sqlx
- tokio
- tower
- PostgreSQL
- RabbitMQ
- Redis
- S3

For frontend developers, we are looking for someone who is familiar with the following technologies:

- Svelte
- SvelteKit
- Astro
- Tailwind CSS 
- TypeScript

Since the development is majorly using Rust and TypeScript, we are looking for someone who is familiar with both languages. The codebase is open source and will be available on GitHub.

Intermediate English proficiency is required.

We will do these to ensure you are a good fit for the position:

1. Check your email to confirm your application.
2. For frontend developers, we will check your open source contributions or projects you have worked on. You need to provide at least one link to your work.
3. For backend developers, we will let you to complete a medium-difficulty LeetCode problem to test your coding skills with 30 minutes limit in C, C++, or Rust. AI is allowed.
4. Based on your response to the above tests, we may do a audio interview to test your skills. The interview will be conducted in English or Japanese. The interview will test your communication skills and your insights into the project.

### Internationalization Staff

Since the VNDB project is in English, while we are open to internationalization, we are looking for someone who is fluent in English and another language and has a good understanding of the VNDB project.

Advanced English proficiency is required. For non-native English speakers, we will use the following standards to evaluate your English proficiency:

- TOEFL: 80 or higher
- IELTS: 6.5 or higher
- TOEIC: 900 or higher

Since most of the visual novels are in Japanese, we are looking for someone who is fluent in Japanese and another language and has a good understanding of the Japanese language and culture.

For non-native Japanese speakers, we suggest you to take the following tests to evaluate your Japanese proficiency:

- JLPT: N1
- BJT: J2 or higher

For privacy concerns, we will not ask you to provide your test scores or certificates. However, we will do a short text interview to test your skills. The interview will be conducted in English or/and Japanese.

Other languages, like Chinese, Spanish, etc., are also welcome. For other languages, either advanced English proficiency or advanced Japanese proficiency is required.

## FAQ

### Is this a full-time position?

No, this is a voluntary position. You can work on it part-time or full-time as you like.

### Will I be compensated?

No, this is a voluntary position. You can work on it part-time or full-time as you like.

### Do I need to apply developer positions to contribute to the codebase?

Maybe not. When the project passes the beta stage, we will open pull request permission for everyone. Before that, only the developers who are selected for the position will be able to contribute to the codebase.

If you apply for developer positions, your account will be given a "Site Developer" badge.

### I have not taken any language tests yet. Can I still apply?

Yes, you can still apply. The tests above are not mandatory, but we let you know the requirements so you can prepare yourself.

### How much time will I need to spend on this project?

It depends on the position you apply for.

For developer positions, in the early stage, you will need to spend some time to code. There is no strict time requirement, and you can pick tasks as you like, but you must complete the tasks you picked in time. After the project passes the beta stage, the working time will be almost zero.

For internationalization staff positions, you will need to spend some time to translate the content and UI. In early stage, we will require you to be online based your timezone. After the project passes the alpha stage, you can work on your own time.

For community moderator, we need you to check the community at least twice a week.

### When will I be notified of the result?

For each step, we will notify you of the result within 1 working day, in Tokyo time.
